Principles

Rooted We try and acknowledge the ties and connections we have with the world.
Community-serving We exist as one amongst others and want to be sharing and in dialogue.
Long-viewed We are oriented towards the long-term.
Explorative We think making unlikely connections are a key part of creative practice.
Networked We and our practice weave between the physical and the digital world.
Small We are and will stay small.

Process

Start small It is easy to get overwhlemed by a project. Better start small and build up, slowly. Especially these days.
One project at a time It is easy to end up working on many projects at the same time. We will for the timebeing work on one project at a time.
Each project should be very different from the previous one As a young practice we are exploring the textures of different media.
